Sequence of Play Per Turn

1. Wandering monsters: Roll when noted in turn tracker. Typically 1 on D6

2. Actions: The party decides what action to take (e.g. moving, searching, listening, entering rooms).

3. Description: Describe what happens. (e.g. Wandering monsters appear, torches expire)

4. End of turn: Tick off a turn.

Time

Rounds = 10 seconds

Minutes = 6 rounds

Turns = 10 minutes

Hours = 6 turns

Common Durations

Torch: 6 turns (1 hour)

Lantern: 24 turns (4 hours)

Light (C): 12 turns (2 hours)

Light (MU): 6 turns + 1/level

Potion: 1d6 + 6 turns

Burning oil pool: 1 turn

Resting (1 Turn/Hour)

Characters who do not rest suffer a –1 penalty to attack and damage rolls until they have rested for one turn.

Actions in 1 Turn

Move at base movement rate.

Search a 10’ × 10’ area: For secret doors / room traps.

Search an item for treasure traps. (Find traps skill)

Encounter: All encounters take 1 turn.

Debate: The referee may rule that a turn has passed while PCs discuss.

Other actions: The referee should rule on which other actions take 1 turn. e.g. attempts to pick a lock, listen at a door, etc.
